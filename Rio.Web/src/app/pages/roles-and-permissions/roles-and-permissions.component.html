Roles and Permissions

Public Users
Public users do not have an account or are not logged into the web application. Public users can only view the homepage and pages under the Learn More menu.
Landowner
Landowners use the platform to manage their water usage and trade water (if trading is enabled in the platform). A user with the Landowner role must be assigned at least one billing account before they can meaningfully use the platform. Landowners can view their Landowner Dashboard and access all items in the Trade Menu. A Landowner cannot view any of the pages under the Manage Menu.
Administrator
Administrators have access to all pages in the application. Administrators can assign other users a role, as well as manage the system (including customizing rich text pages). The Administrator role should be limited to a few users who understand the tool and need access to add/edit accounts and manage user roles.
Demo User
A Demo User has similar access as a Landowner and must be assigned at least one billing account to start trading. However, they have additional read-only permission to view all pages in the Manage Menu.
Unassigned
Unassigned users have a system account but have not been assigned a role by an Administrator. These users have the same permissions as Public users. A new user who signs up on their own will automatically be assigned this role.
Disabled
Disabled users have the same permissions as Public and Unassigned users. An administrator may assign this role to users who created an account by accident, or who no longer have a valid reason to remain in the platform - for example a former Landowner who has left the district, or a previous employee who is no longer with your organization.
