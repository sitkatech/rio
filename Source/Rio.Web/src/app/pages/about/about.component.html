1. Platform Overview

Platform Overview

Rosedale-Rio Bravo Water Storage District (“Rosedale”) has partnered with Environmental Defense Fund, Sitka Technology Group, OpenET, and WestWater Research to develop an online water accounting platform. The accounting platform was designed for Rosedale and its landowners to facilitate the effective accounting and management of available water resources within the district, to help Rosedale achieve groundwater sustainability goals, and to minimize economic costs to the community.

Environmental Defense Fund

Environmental Defense Fund, a leading international nonprofit organization, links science, economics, law and innovative private-sector partnerships to create transformational solutions to the most serious environmental problems.

Sitka Technology Group

Sitka develops knowledge infrastructures for conservation, restoration, and sustainable development. The infrastructure systems Sitka designs with clients deliver three types of value: realizing operational efficiencies, tracking performance against social and ecological goals, and turning field data into actionable intelligence. Simply put, Sitka’s solutions enable decision makers to be more effective in making decisions.

OpenET

OpenET provides access to more transparent, broadly trusted estimates of water use (evapotranspiration, or ET) using publicly available data from multiple satellites and weather stations. The OpenET project team includes leading national and international experts in remote sensing of ET, cloud computing, water policy, and water markets, partnered with nationally recognized web development teams and leaders in the western agriculture and water management communities. Rosedale has partnered with the OpenET project to understand how data on evapotranspiration and consumptive use can be utilized to support implementation of the Sustainable Groundwater Management Act in ways that provide value to all stakeholders.
